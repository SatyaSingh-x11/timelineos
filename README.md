#timelineos

A personal archive of growth, projects, milestones, failures, experiments, and becoming.

TimelineOS is not a blog.
It is not a portfolio.

It is a living timeline — documented slowly, honestly, and publicly.

---

Philosophy

The internet usually shows polished outcomes.

TimelineOS exists to document:

- the process,
- the learning,
- the mistakes,
- the experiments,
- the evolution.

Every month becomes part of the archive.

---

Structure

index.html        → Landing page
may.html          → Monthly timeline
june.html         → Monthly timeline
twoktwosix.html   → Full year archive

Future pages will contain:

- milestone cards
- projects
- lessons
- failures
- screenshots
- ideas
- progress logs

Design

- Mobile responsive

---

Tech Stack

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript

Just a lightweight personal system.

Purpose
