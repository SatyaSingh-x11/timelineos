About TimelineOS

Not a portfolio. More like a memory system.

TimelineOS is a personal archive documenting projects, ideas, experiments, failures, milestones, and growth over time. Built slowly by Satya as a way to make progress visible.

Philosophy

Progress disappears when you don't document it.

Most projects vanish. Ideas get forgotten. Growth becomes invisible. TimelineOS exists to preserve the process instead of only the polished outcomes.
